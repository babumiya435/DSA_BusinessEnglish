** Part Of Speech **

The Oxford Learner’s Dictionary defines parts of speech as 
    “one of the classes into which words are divided according to their grammar, such as noun, verb, adjective, etc.”

The Cambridge Dictionary also gives a similar definition – 
    “One of the grammatical groups into which words are divided, such as noun, verb, and adjective etc.”.

There are eight parts of speech to which a word can belong

1. Nouns
2. Pronouns
3. Verbs
4. Adverbs
5. Adjectives
6. Prepositions
7. Conjusctions
8. Interjections
